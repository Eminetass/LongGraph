"1984" is a novel by George Orwell. Winston Smith lives in Oceania under the oppressive regime of Big Brother. Julia and Winston rebel against the Party's control.

"The Great Gatsby" is written by F. Scott Fitzgerald. Jay Gatsby is in love with Daisy Buchanan. The story takes place in West Egg and East Egg.
"Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone" is a novel by J.K. Rowling. Harry Potter, a wizard, attends Hogwarts and battles Voldemort.
