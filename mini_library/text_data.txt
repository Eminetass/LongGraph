"1984" is a novel by George Orwell. It is set in a dystopian future where Winston Smith lives under the oppressive regime of Big Brother in Oceania. Winston begins a secret affair with Julia and questions the Party's authority.

"The Great Gatsby" is written by F. Scott Fitzgerald. The story revolves around Jay Gatsby, who is in love with Daisy Buchanan. Most events take place in West Egg and East Egg, where Gatsby throws lavish parties.
